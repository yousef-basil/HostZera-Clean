# 🎨 Frontend Development Guide (HostZera)

Welcome! This is your dedicated zone for UI/UX and Design.

### 📂 Your Main Files:
- **Views (HTML/Blade):** `resources/views/front/`
  - *Keep your pages here to avoid mixing with Admin dashboard.*
- **Layouts:** `resources/views/layouts/`
- **Styles (CSS):** `public/css/` or `resources/css/`
- **Scripts (JS):** `public/js/`
- **Images/Icons:** `public/images/` or `public/assets/`

### 💡 Tips:
- Use **Lucide Icons** as we standardized.
- The color theme is **Dark Mode** with **Royal Purple (#8b5cf6)** accents.
- Always check the `home.blade.php` for the general style reference.
